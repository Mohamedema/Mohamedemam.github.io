About

I am a PhD researcher at CIIMAR, University of Porto / IST, University of Lisbon, working on graph-based neural network models for multi-omics data integration to analyze large-scale cancer cell line datasets. My research sits at the intersection of machine learning, bioinformatics, and computational oncology. I am funded by the la Caixa Foundation (Inphinit Fellowship) and FCT (PhD Studentship 2025.01385.BD).
